When dealing with color it is important to take calibration into account. In this case, being a online test, we can't control all the conditions where you are performing the test, so we have to use an alternative that will be based on your answers to the following 2 questions. First, we will present an image with 10 different squares on a black background, with different shades of black, a red number and a correspondent word below them. We ask you to to write in the according boxes below the last perceived number and word that you can see. If you cannot detect any word or square please write the number 0 and the word "None".
