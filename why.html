Why do I need to tell you this?

All the information retrieved is merely for statistical purposes, however below, I briefly explain the reasons for each question:

  • Age - Color perception changes with age, so age information is important to help us conceive user profiles. See: Color vision problems become more common with age.

  • Gender - A different gender has a different response to the same color. See: The Meaning of Color for Gender.

  • Nationality - The country were you are born can give you different meanings when comparing with other countries. See Colours in Cultures.

  • Country where you lived most of your life - Different cultures have different meanings for each color, so the place where you live most of your time can influence your color meaning perceptions. See: Colours in Cultures.

  • Native Language - Language can shape color perception. See: Hues and views.
